What is a double-hung window?

Double-hung windows are composed of two movable sashes that are slightly offset from one another, which allows you to open and close vertically within a window farm. The upper can be lowered and the bottom can be raised to create ventilation. The window sashes tilt, so you can easily reach the outside surface for cleaning as needed.

Why choose double-hung windows?

  • Convenience They are easy to clean, convenient to operate and offer excellent ventilation
  • Comfortdouble-hung windows provide you with superior weather tightness to guard against drafts and the elements, and they can reduce outdoor noises.
  • Simple Operation The sashes on double-hung windows are counterbalanced, making them easy to open and close with minimal effort.
  • Appearance Double-hung windows can add elegance to your home with their traditional appearance and are suitable for any home décor.
  • SecurityAdvanced locking systems and the ability to ventilate using the upper sash help keep your home safe and secure
